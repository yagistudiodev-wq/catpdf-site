Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

CatPDF ("the App", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles and how.

1. Data we collect

CatPDF does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or to any third party. The App does not currently use analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash-reporting services.

2. How your files are processed

All conversions (PDF, JPG, PNG, WEBP) happen entirely on your device. The files you select are never uploaded to a server or seen by us — they stay on your phone throughout the entire conversion process.

3. Permissions

CatPDF requests access to your device's storage solely so you can select files to convert and save the converted results. This access is used only for that purpose and for nothing else.

4. Advertising

CatPDF does not currently show advertisements. If we introduce advertising in a future update, this Privacy Policy will be updated in advance to describe what data (if any) is collected by the advertising provider and how it is used, before that update is released.

5. Children's privacy

CatPDF does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect personal data at all.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: yagistudio.dev@gmail.com
